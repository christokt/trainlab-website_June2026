PhD Openings

We will have openings for 1–2 PhD students during 2026–2027. We are looking for highly motivated and talented students with a strong background in at least two areas among AI, wireless communication, mathematical optimization, or Bayesian inference.

WPI has rolling admission, so students are welcome to apply anytime during the year.

How to apply

Please reach out with your CV, publications if any, and transcripts.

We are especially interested in students passionate about reshaping the future of wireless communications through rigorous research and innovation.

Email TRAIN Lab

What to expect

Life in the TRAIN Lab blends theory, systems, and real-world deployment — from mathematical foundations and AI/ML to building and testing on real wireless platforms. We value curiosity, rigor, and ownership: you’ll be encouraged to develop independent research directions, publish at top venues, and present your work at conferences. Expect close mentorship, collaborative discussion, and a focus on understanding why things work, not just that they do.
